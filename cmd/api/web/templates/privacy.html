Privacy Policy

Last updated: {{.Year}}

What we collect

When you shorten a URL we store the original URL, a short code, your user ID (if logged in), and metadata such as creation time and expiration.

When someone follows a short link we record a click event containing the timestamp, the referring URL, an anonymized country/device breakdown, and a hashed version of the IP (SHA-256 with a rotating server-side salt). We never store raw IP addresses.

What we don't collect

  • No raw IP addresses.
  • No third-party tracking scripts or ad SDKs.
  • No profile of your browsing history across other sites.

Retention

Click event records auto-expire after 90 days via DynamoDB TTL. Link records expire when their TTL or max-click limit is reached. Account data is deleted on request — contact us.

Your rights

You can request a copy, correction, or deletion of your data at any time via our contact page.
